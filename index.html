What Are We?

The **Global Career Network (GCN)** at Arizona State University is a student-led organization dedicated to empowering international students for success in the global job market. We understand the unique challenges faced by international students, from navigating visas (CPT/OPT/H-1B) to adapting to U.S. recruitment culture.

Through structured peer mentoring, industry collaborations, and skill development workshops, we build a bridge between academic learning and high-impact career opportunities in top multinational corporations.
